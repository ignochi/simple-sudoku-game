Random Fact

There are over 5 billion valid Sudoku puzzles, and it would take a lifetime to solve them all.

Sudoku is a popular, logic-based number puzzle. The name Sudoku is an abbreviation of the Japanese phrase, Sūji wa dokushin ni kagiru (数字は独身に限る), which can be translated as "the digits must be single".

A standard Sudoku puzzle consists of a 9×9 grid (9 rows and 9 columns) with 3×3 subgrids. There are other variations of the Sudoku puzzle. These include the 4×4 puzzle, the 6×6 puzzle, and a host of others.

The standard rule of Sudoku is simple: you are required to fill each cell with a number so that each column, each row, and each of the subgrids that compose the grid (also called "boxes," "blocks," or "regions") contains all of the digits from 1 to n, n being the number of cells in a row. Some cells have been filled by default, you cannot change the values of these cells.

Contrary to popular belief, Sudoku is logic-based, and does not require any arithmetic. It is a mind-bending, strategic adventure. Sudoku is the perfect mental workout – it sharpens your focus and enhances problem-solving skills.

Input values

Each puzzle has a keyboard on the screen. To input a number in a cell:

  1. Click or focus on the cell.
  2. Click the keyboard button that has the value of your choice.

If your device uses a keyboard, in addition to the steps above, you can execute the steps listed below:

  1. Click or focus on a cell.
  2. Press the number on your device's keyboard that has the value of your choice.

Clear the puzzle

To restart a particular puzzle, click the restart button on your screen. If your device uses a keyboard, press the "r" key on your keyboard.

Exit a puzzle

To exit a puzzle, click the 'back' key at the top-left corner of the page.
If your device uses a keyboard, pressing the 'Escape' (Esc) key produces the same result.
You should know that you don't lose your inputs when you exit a page unless you restart the puzzle or exit the game entirely.

The purpose of this game is to dispel any misconceptions you might have about Sudoku by showing you how easy and fun it is.
